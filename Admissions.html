Admissions

Bishop Allison Theological College offers students the opportunity to develop their character, knowledge, and leadership abilities through its combined focus on spiritual formation and rigorous academic study. Interested students are encouraged to apply through their church leadership, and churches can obtain application forms from the office of the Academic Dean or from the link below.

After collecting the application form, prospective students must fill the form in its entirety and attach the required photocopies and documents. Please see the link below for the recommendation form for referees. Referees must use the recommendation form supplied by BATC for their recommendations for prospective students.

In order to be considered for admission, prospective students must ensure that their application forms with the attached documents have been submitted to the office of the Academic Dean by December 5th. Intake for all students will occur at the beginning of Trinity Semester which takes place from May through August. Churches will be notified concerning the admission status of their students by March 1st.
