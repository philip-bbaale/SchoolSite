Partnership with Duke Divinity School

As part of their field education program, Duke Divinity School sends Master of Divinity students to come and teach at Bishop Allison during our Trinity Semester. This partnership enables both the students from Duke and our students at Bishop Allison to develop a stronger sense of the global body of Christ.
