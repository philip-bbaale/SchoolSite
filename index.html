Bishop Allison Theological College

A Christian Center of Academic Excellence and Deep Spirituality in the Heart of South Sudan and Africa

Our Mission

The mission of Bishop Allison Theological College is to train Christian leaders to serve the churches and communities of South Sudan. Our programs are designed to prepare our students both spiritually and academically in order to equip them for a lifetime of service to our Lord Jesus Christ.

Bishop Allison Theological College has made me to be able to deliver good services to the people, and people are getting something which is helpful in their lives. The studies have helped me personally and have helped my community.

Rev. Peter Ale,

BATC student from the Episcopal Diocese of Yei

At BATC I am able to study as a young woman. Through my studies, I have learned things about church which I did not know, even though I was a Christian. Also, BATC has helped me because I was in the refugee camp living with trauma. But in BATC we had trauma healing classes, and I became well.

Aidah Elly,

BATC student from Africa Inland Church, Yei

BATC has transformed my life. When people see where I came from, they will see the difference in my life. I am now able to speak to the people, counsel people, and even help people in difficult times such as funerals.

Vita Mambo,

BATC student from the Episcopal Diocese of Morobo
