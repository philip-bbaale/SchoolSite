Courses Offered

Certificate in Theology

The certificate in theology course is a 2-year course which will prepare students for ministry in their churches and communities. In order to qualify for the certificate in theology course, students must have passed the South Sudan Certificate of Education Examinations, O-Level Exams in Uganda, or their equivalent.

List of Course Units for Certificate in Theology

Diploma in Theology

The diploma in theology course is the more advanced of our courses. This course is a 3-year course, and it also functions to prepare students for ministry in their churches and communities. In order to qualify for the diploma in theology course, students must meet the following two requirements. First, they must have passed the South Sudan Certificate of Education Examinations, O-Level Examinations in Uganda, or their equivalent. And, second, they must have completed a certificate-level course in a recognized college or university or have earned at least one Principal Pass and two Subsidiary Passes for A-Level Exams in Uganda.
