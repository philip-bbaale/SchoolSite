Student Life

At BATC students experience a vibrant community in which they are able to grow spiritually and develop friendships with other future leaders of the churches and communities in South Sudan. Our community at the college is central to the formation of our students and to their preparation for ministry, and our graduates have noted how the friendships they developed at BATC have helped them throughout their years of ministry. As part of the BATC community, students are able to participate in various extracurricular activities at the college. The following are some of the extracurricular activities in which students at BATC are able to participate.

Guild Council

Students form and participate in their own student government. The student government oversees all aspects of student life at the college and interacts directly with the college's administration and board of governors to advocate on the students' behalf

BATC Choir

Students are able to participate in the BATC choir. In addition to singing within the college, the BATC choir has been invited to perform for various local worship services.

Football

Students are also able to participate in football at the college. In addition to daily recreation sessions, students also play friendly matches against local teams.

Agriculture

Students work together to grow food for the college. Working under the supervision of the Guild Council's Ministry of Agriculture, students contribute to the work of the college by providing food for themselves and for the staff of the college.
